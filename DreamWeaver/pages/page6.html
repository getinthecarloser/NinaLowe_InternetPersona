     By the end of the day they reached the edge of the field. It led into a forest that they all knew very well. The setting sun shone through the trees, accentuating the many freckles covering Cleo’s face. She looked up at Elliot with her eyes wide. Elliot nodded and she noticed for the first time how nice his dark brown hair looked in contrast with his light eyes. It wasn’t as harsh as Evan’s jet black locks. They started into the forest.


Nikolai’s gaze kept wandering towards Evan and he didn’t notice the tree.


“Ouff,” Nik exclaimed. “What the fuck is this?”


“Language!” said Elliot. “Cleo’s only 12.” He looked down at her next to him and smiled.


“I’m fourteen…” Cleo said quietly, while pushing her blonde hair behind her left ear.


“Guys! We really need to keep moving!” Evan yelled from up ahead. “What are you even doing back there?”

 
“What are you doing, where are you even taking us?!” Nik yelled back. Evan turned around and walked back towards Nik with her eyes so focused on his face anything in her line of vision could be burnt by her gaze. She kicked him in the nuts, her face as blank as an unwritten story. Nikolai doubled over letting out short breaths. His eyebrows tightened in anger as if being pulled down by two strings and his teeth clenched.


“I told you not to ask that again,” she said shrugging her shoulders, tilting her head to the side and with her bottom lip slightly protruding with nonchalance.  “Come on, get up, let’s go,” said Evan, extending her hand to help him up. In spite of the situation, he took it.
