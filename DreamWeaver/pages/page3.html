Internet Persona

 

 

   

“Why are you all so calm about this?” asked Nik. “You know your brother is the one that caused this to happen Evangeline.”


“Don’t, call me that! And I don’t know anything. Look behind you, it’s all just gray, like nothing was ever there. How could this possibly be Elliot’s fault? He’s only 15. No matter what he says, this was not caused by him.”


“You know I’m right behind you guys right?” said Elliot. “Plus I never said I caused this. I simply made a connection between the time I pulled the thread and the time the smoke showed up.”


Evan’s long black hair blew into Nik’s face as she turned to look at Elliot. “Honestly Elliot, as if the pulling of a golden thread out of a magical comforter delivered to us from the gods would bring down the fury of a hungry smoke unto our land.”

Cleo raised her eyebrow at Evan and slowly shook her head. Evan was always so dramatic, she thought.


“Yes I know Cleo, I was emphasizing the ridiculousness of his ‘connection’,” Evan said, noticing Cleo’s judgment.


“But I pulled the loose thread out of my blanket at the exact same time that we heard the first scream,” said Elliot. As Elliot mentioned the scream, Nikolai thought back to that day.
