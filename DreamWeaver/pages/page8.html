Internet Persona

 

   

     “Cleo!” Elliot said for the fourth time, thrusting some meat forward. Cleo looked away from the fire and up at Elliot holding the unappetizing slice of charred animal. Cleo shook her head and said no thank you.  Elliot sat down next to her with his portion of prairie dog and began biting into it like he hadn’t eaten in weeks.


“Aw Elliot that’s so gross,” said Evan. She picked up a handful of leaves at her foot and tossed them in Elliot’s face. Nikolai was sitting cross legged on the opposite side of the fire. His eyes widened and Evan turned her head to see what he was looking at behind them. The smoke was back and was consuming the trees, leaves, dirt and everything that was swept into its shiny cloud.


“It’s advancing at a slower pace than before,” Cleo told them. “We should be okay here for tonight, but should get moving early in the morning.”  She scooched her bum forward on the log Nikolai had laid down when they got there, and took out the mini note pad she always kept in her back pocket.


“What are you always writing in that thing?” asked Elliot leaning over her shoulder to take a look. Cleo turned her head to look at him as she replied but she didn’t realize how close his face was. She quickly looked back down at her pen.


“Recording its appearances and patterns for the scientists if we ever get out of here,” she said.  Evan chuckled to herself and looked up at Nik. Nik was looking back at her with a knowing smirk. They had both caught a glimpse of it when she dropped it that morning and the wind made it hop forward on the ground. There was a page with a large heart with Elliot’s name inside of it.
