Internet Persona

 

   

     The four of them met five days ago right after the initial attack. The smoke, made up of small twinkling lights traveling on a gray wind, came down slowly and everyone thought it was a beautiful phenomenon; until it touched down and anything it made contact with vanished into thin air. Not only people, but entire buildings were disappearing. All that was left was a wall of gray. Evan Potestas and her brother Elliot, Cleo Clemens and Nikolai Odiosus made it to an open field near the edge of town. Together they began walking as far away from the all-consuming grayness as possible.
