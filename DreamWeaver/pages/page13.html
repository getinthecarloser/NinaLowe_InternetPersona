Internet Persona

 

   

     Time passed and Evan felt nothing; she opened her eyes and they were surrounded by the smoke. Everywhere they looked, from all sides, nothing was there. It was like looking out into a blank computer screen but they were surrounded by it. The smoke was making contact with their skin now but they stayed put and they could still all be seen by one another. They were the only things left however. The small shiny particles of the gray cloud pulled upwards on their arm hairs. Cleo looked up from her position on the ground and saw there was a clearing directly above them; a perfect circle where the smoke did not touch.
