Internet Persona

 

   

     Evan woke up without being able to breath. She felt like she couldn’t get enough air in her lungs like there was a heavy weight on her chest. She looked down to see that Nik moved closer in the night and used her as his pillow.


“Get off me,” she grumbled through her teeth as she lifted his head and pushed it to the dirt.


“Ow! Was that necessary?”


“Shit, guys look, the smoke is deeper into the forest. We’ve got to move!” said Evan. She moved towards Elliot and Cleo and gave them little nudges to wake them up. They both looked confused. Nik just pointed towards the smoke and they both jumped up and knew what was going on. They started running deeper into the forest staying close together.


“How do you know where to go?” Nikolai asked while breathing heavily.


“I don’t, I just know we have to keep moving away from that,” she replied, pointing her chin briefly behind her. They reached the other side of the forest but there was no real way out. The edge of the treeline was right next to a rock face. Evan worked her way to the right and brought them to an opening in the rock. Nikolai recognized the cave as the place he used to explore when he was ten, with his friend Thomas. Before he moved in with his mom, he lived next to Cross Pike Mountain with his dad. He didn’t realize those were the woods they were in.
