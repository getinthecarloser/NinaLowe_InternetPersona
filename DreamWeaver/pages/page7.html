Internet Persona

 

   

.....Cleo led them to a clearing in the forest where the leaves on the ground were flattened in a perfect circle as if an alien spaceship had landed there moments earlier. She told them this is the spot she used to camp with her family. Evan and Elliot both used to camp in these woods as well with their parents and older brother Tate. Elliot started a fire to cook up the prairie dogs and they all settled around it. The mixture of the smoke and the roasting of the animals made an awful smell that Cleo found familiar. Staring into the fire, the sun having fully set, she thought back to the day the smoke arrived.


Cleo’s little sister Anabel had been playing with her dolls in the living room. Her parents were gone to a meeting with the bank and she was in charge of making dinner for Anabel and herself. She had done it many times before and today she was going to make chicken and rice. It was her sister’s favorite meal and her mom never thought to make it. The rice had been cooking in the rice cooker for a bit and she was now going to put the chicken in a skillet.  She picked the slimy uncooked chicken up and placed it in the hot pan. While she was wiping her hands off on a towel Anabel attempted to put one of the Barbie’s shoes in her mouth. Rushing to make sure her sister didn’t choke, Cleo threw the towel down quickly. She took the Barbie’s shoe out of Anabel’s hand and took the other shoe off the Barbie for good measure. Then she smelt smoke and her eyes began to water. Cleo rapidly turned to look at the chicken. The chicken wasn’t burning but the towel she threw down had caught fire. Cleo rushed back over to turn the stove off and then find the apartment’s fire extinguisher, but when she got to the stove she heard a pop and felt the burning of the sun in her face. The very next second her face cooled down and she opened her eyes to see the stove had disappeared and all the rooms behind the kitchen were gone. All she saw was gray and a smoky substance filled the air.  She heard Anabel from behind her.


“Cleo? Cleo?” The gray smoke was approaching her sister. “Cleeeeo?” her sister said looking down at her Ken doll that she was making talk to the female Barbie, whom she had named Cleo.
